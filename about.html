What is Project 30?

Project 30 is inspired by the former band 30 Minutes, formed by the 3 members of The Lemonade Rhapsody: Bathan, Gian, and Enad. We are looking for more members as our band members are not official yet.





    Our Goals for Project 30
  • Earn the respect of the school
  • Enjoy and perform to the fullest
  • Be remembered as a good band to other future Bosconians
  • To perform gigs despite being our last year
  • Give the audience the hype and so for ourselves
  • Inspire other Bosconians to create their own band
  • No matter how hard, We strive to be better
  • To Improve and learn from the mistakes from our performance
  • Create the best of the best memories
  • Understand and respect on one another
